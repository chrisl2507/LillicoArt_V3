Who We Are

Lillico Art is a sole-trader studio based in the United Kingdom. This website is operated by Lillico Art ("we", "us", "our").

What Data We Collect

When you use our contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your message content
  • Any additional details you choose to provide (commission type, timeline)

We do not collect any additional data via the contact form beyond what you provide.

Cookies & Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics to collect anonymised data about how visitors interact with the site — including pages visited, time on site, and device type. This data helps us understand how people engage with the work and improve the site experience.

Google Analytics uses cookies to collect this information. No personally identifiable information is sent to Google. You can opt out at any time using Google's opt-out browser add-on or by declining cookies when prompted on this site.

Form submission data (name, email, message) is handled separately by Netlify and is not connected to analytics. See Netlify's privacy policy for details.

How We Use Your Data

The information you provide via the contact form is used solely to respond to your enquiry or commission request. We will not share your information with third parties, use it for marketing, or store it beyond what is necessary to fulfil your request.

Form Processing

Contact form submissions are processed by Netlify. Netlify's privacy policy applies to the handling of form data during submission. You can read Netlify's privacy policy on their website.

Your Rights

Under UK data protection law (UK GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at lilli.co.art@gmail.com.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please get in touch at lilli.co.art@gmail.com.
